Brief History

BOOLERZ Limited began the year 2021. Since its incorporation, the company has undertaken several challenging tendering projects and accumulated skills, know-how and experiences in Graphic and design, Online services, system data management, stationaries supplies, fresh fruits and food supplies, Sports and Games Equipment, among others.
Today, BOOLERZ Limited Company takes on the role for small to medium size supplies and management services to coordinate specialist traders for general supplies. We also provide printing on papers and other materials and publishing services. Our objective is to provide services to our clients with the value of their money when we are chosen to execute their projects. Our emphasis is on clear communication and follow-up procedures to ensure that client’s objectives are top priority in the planning and execution of all our processes. Our project management and execution philosophy is:

 create detailed schedule and resources to meet client’s project objective,

 communicate clearly with all project stakeholders,

 Track delivery process.

 Supply and deliver goods and services on time.

We take pride in our delivery, thus our clients can always be assured that only the most experienced and qualified people are serving them, all the time. Our Vision: To be a respectable general supplier delivering beyond expectation, always.

Our Mission: To procure projects at competitive pricing, provide safe handling of commodities and deliver quality goods and services within reasonable time frame. BOOLERZ Limited, incorporated in the republic of Kenya. We are registered with The Public Procurement Opportunity (AGPO) and the county Government and licensed to serve of our customer’s needs. We are based in Mariakani but we operate throughout the country with a strong team of professionals and some of the best skilled manpower. BOOLERZ Limited is under one full time director who has professional and practical experience in the supply industry for over the years. We offer array of goods & services that include the following but not limited to: - Graphic and design, Online services, system data management, stationaries supplies, Sports and Games Equipment, fresh fruits and food supplies. At BOOLERZ Limited we believe in tailoring our services to uniquely fit our clients’ needs, both in terms of function and budget.
